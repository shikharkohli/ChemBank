Post Result Stress

Some students tend to become anxious as the result date is announced and it continues till the result is declared. It is important to remember that you have tried your best.

Be content with what you get.

And then, plan and build your future.

Students

Have objective assessment of your result.

Evaluate your options, regarding admissions to the next class/course

Have realistic goals

Maintain positive outlook

Be optimistic

For Parents

Have realistic expectations

Keep calm, whatever be the result

Always motivate your child

Participate in discussions regarding your child’s future

Don’t worry about social norms and expectations

Do not compare your child with any other child, each child is unique

Be optimistic
