Exam Time

Before Exam

Do not procrastinate.

Know the syllabus of the exams and accordingly prepare your own notes and collect study material.

Have your text-books handy.

Make your own notes, preferably hand written.

For better memory recall, take proper night rest before exam day.

Take balanced diet.

Keep adequate time for revision.

Keep away from caffeine products (tea, coffee, etc.) or cold drinks or energy drinks. Intead, take lime water or green tea.

Do not stay awake the night before the exam as it can make you feel tired or sleepy during exam time.

Arrange you pencil box, stationary items a day before the exam.

During Exam

Most students experience some anxiety or stress when they enter the examination room. This is normal and is a type of positive stress. Some amount of anxiety can help you perform better.

Get your own stationary items.

Take your own watch with accurate time, to avoid distractions.

Listen to verbal instructions of the invigilator carefully.

Read instructions in the question paper carefully.

It is not necessary to answer questions in the given serial order. You can answer the easiest question first.

In case you forget an answer, breath deep and put in your best effort to recall the answer.
