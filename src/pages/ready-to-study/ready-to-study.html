Ready To Study

Tips

Developing good study habits
goes a long way in relieving
examination stress.

Time management

Plan your study time and try to stick to your plan. Set up a time-table and study accordingly. Ask your teacher to help you, in case you find it tough to prepare a time-table.

Study Space

Organize your study space well by having proper light and air space. Avoid clutter on your study table. Also, have proper and comfortable study table and chair. There should no noise while you study as it could be distract you.

Break Time

Never stretch you break time during. Return to your study zone, as soon as your break time is over.

Sample Question Papers

Always keep sample question papers handy and refer to them for marking scheme and sample answers.

For subjects like Maths, Science, prepare a list of formulae which are your ready reckoner

and you can glance them often. For Social Science, prepare time-lines to remember dates. Write important facts on Sticky Notes or Index Cards and keep these at prominent places where you can revise them often.

Group Study

For certain topics which you feel difficult, you can take up group study. Also, take up discussion and brain storming sessions with your peer group.

Do not cram

Avoid rote memory and focus on understanding the topic. This way you will retain the concepts for a longer time. Organise the content into mind maps or pictures or key words.

Teach it

Teaching others cements learning. During breaks or during revision time, try telling you family what you have revised.
